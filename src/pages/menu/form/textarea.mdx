## `data-primary`

设置 `[data-primary]` 可以覆盖全局css变量 `--outline-color-focus`

<Grid inline>
    <T tag="textarea" class="☘-textarea" title="primary" placeholder='primary' data-primary />
    <T tag="textarea" class="☘-textarea" title="success" placeholder='success' data-primary="success" />
    <T tag="textarea" class="☘-textarea" title="danger" placeholder='danger' data-primary="danger" />
    <T tag="textarea" class="☘-textarea" title="warning" placeholder='warning' data-primary="warning" />
</Grid>

```pug
each primary in ['', 'success', 'danger', 'warning']
    textarea.☘-textarea(title=primary placeholder=primary data-primary=primary)
```

## `.sm` & `.lg`

不设置宽高时默认是2行高20个等宽半角字符宽

全局变量 `--padding-textarea` 设置内边距

有下面两个预设尺寸：

- `textarea.☘-textarea.sm`
    <br/><T tag="textarea" class="☘-textarea sm" title="尺寸更小的文本域" placeholder="更小" />
- `textarea.☘-textarea.lg`
    <br/><T tag="textarea" class="☘-textarea lg" title="尺寸更大的文本域" placeholder="更大" />
